Alexis Denise Williams

Alexis is a creative technologist specializing in developing web environments for multimedia projects. As a Black and Latina queer woman, Williams has a passion for working with nonprofits on social justice causes. One of her projects PB-Resources, a web based toolkit for the Black Lives movement, has reached over 2 million users.
