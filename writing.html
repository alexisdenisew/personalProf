Creative Writing Poetry Collection, 2020

My most recent polished piece of work. A collection of poetry for my Fall 2020 Creative Writing class. I like them a lot, and these pieces are some of my most fun and experimental recent works.

The Short List of Things I'm in Love With (Part 1), 2019

Written in 2019 right before starting college. Reflecting on the parts of life and the world I'm in love with. I love coming back to this piece to remind my self of the simplicity of life and myself. It keeps me grounded. Also a fan favorite for some reason.

Its Trendy, and its Black, 2018

For an English class I took senior year of High School. I'm not sure that I still agree with all of it, I think I was being too lenient if I'm honest, but it's a good example of my argument skills and complex relationship with race, culture and identity.
