About Clueless

Clueless with Alexis is a chaos-but-make-it-educational webseries following host Alexis Williams as she stumbles, laughs, and stress-googles her way through “how to be an adult” in New York City. From surviving MTA meltdowns and broker fees to figuring out dating, money, mental health, and whether she’s even registered to vote, Alexis uses her own life as the syllabus for the questions everyone has but no one wants to admit out loud.

A multi-hyphenate creative, coder, and activist, Alexis built her platform by making smart, funny content that doesn’t shy away from real issues facing Gen-Z, especially marginalized communities. In Clueless with Alexis, she breaks down how politics and power quietly shape everyday life, while still feeling like your brutally honest friend FaceTiming you from the subway. Each episode mixes vlog-style storytelling, interviews, and deep dives so viewers walk away feeling a little less clueless and a lot more in control.
