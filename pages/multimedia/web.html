Web Development

PB-Resources

PBR is a web based toolkit created to make it easier for the everyday person to get involved with the social justice movement. It started as a place to pool police brutality resources, but has grown to touch many areas in the movement for Black Lives in a multi-media fashion. Programs: HTML/CSS, JS, in tandem with other external developer apps. Reached 2M users.

The Sexbook

The Sex Book is the web companion to the instagram-based diverse and equitable sex education resource. The team's mission is to bring sex education to underrepresented groups who are often left out of the conversation when sex ed is discussed in more formal settings, like school. Programs: HTML/CSS, JS, in tandem with other external developer apps.
